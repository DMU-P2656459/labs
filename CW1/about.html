Synopsis

In Los Angeles, California, a gang of bank robbers known as The Ex-Presidents commit their crimes while wearing masks of former Presidents Ronald Reagan, Jimmy Carter, Richard Nixon, and Lyndon B. Johnson. The F.B.I. suspects that the gang members are surfers and sends young Agent Johnny Utah undercover at the beach to mix with the surfers and gather information. Utah meets surfer Bodhi and is drawn into his new friend's lifestyle.
